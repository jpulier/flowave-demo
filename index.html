Questions? Here are the ones we hear most.

Does it cost anything?

▼
Nothing. Ever. Consultations are completely free. If you move forward with a case, attorneys only get paid if you win — it's called a contingency fee.

What if the accident wasn't 100% my fault?

▼
You may still qualify. In most states, you can recover compensation even if you were partially at fault. Fill out the form and we'll sort it out.

What if it happened a while ago?

▼
You might still have time. Most states give you 2 years from the date of the accident. Don't wait — the sooner you check, the better.

Do I have to talk to a lawyer right away?

▼
No. We'll first reach out to answer your questions and explain your options. Zero pressure.

What if my injuries seem minor?

▼
Minor injuries can still lead to significant compensation, especially for medical costs, missed work, and pain and suffering. It's always worth checking.
